ABOUT

About Me


My journey into design started in an unexpected place: market research. While digging into consumer behavior and data trends, I realized I wanted to do more than just analyze problems—I wanted to solve them. That’s when I discovered UX, a field that perfectly bridges my analytical mindset with my love for creativity. I fell for its multidisciplinary nature—where psychology, storytelling, and pixels collide to create meaningful experiences.


Though I started with a focus on visual design (thanks to endless hours tinkering in Figma and Adobe Suite!), I quickly grew obsessed with the bigger picture: understanding users, refining interactions, and designing systems that scale. Today, I’m passionate about accessibility and ethical design—because great experiences shouldn’t exclude anyone.

Beyond the screen, I enjoy staying active through sports and exercise, exploring new cuisines, unwinding with TV shows and movies, and quality time with family and friends.
